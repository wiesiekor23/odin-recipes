Stone Cold Stinger

Description

This stone-cold stinger shot with Goldschlager, Jagermeister, and peppermint schnapps is sure to get the party started.

Ingredients

  • ½ fluid ounce cinnamon schnapps (i.e. Goldschlager™)
  • ½ fluid ounce peppermint schnapps
  • ½ fluid ounce Jagermeister liqueur
    •

    Steps

    1. Gather all ingredients.
    2. Pour cinnamon schnapps, peppermint schnapps, and Jagermeister into a frozen shot glass.
    3. Enjoy!
