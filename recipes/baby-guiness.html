Baby Guinness

Description

A baby Guinness shot that looks like a mini pint of Guinness. This is a delicious after-dinner cocktail that I learned how to make in London.

Ingredients

  • 2 fluid ounces coffee flavored liqueur
  • Â½ fluid ounce Irish cream liqueur

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Fill a shot glass almost to the top with coffee liqueur.
  3. Top off with Irish cream; it will float on top, making the shot's ''head."
  4. Enjoy!
